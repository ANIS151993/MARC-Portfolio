Research Profile

My work investigates how to make cloud-native security systems practical at enterprise scale. I focus on intelligent firewalls, cross-cloud adaptation, federated threat intelligence, and continuous verification mechanisms for Zero-Trust environments. I combine research design, implementation, and evaluation to produce systems that are both academically rigorous and operationally deployable.
